But... what is ForeverFOMO?

Sick and tired of all those earn and reflection tokens? You're at the right spot here. ForeverFOMO is A price-elastic tokens with adjusting supply, moving towards the price of Bitcoin. Different then just a normal pegged token/stable coin, rebases make price-elastic tokens into synthetic commodities with fluctuating values and supplies that gradually stabilize. Ultimately, rebases are designed to be tradable and potentially extremely profitable.

When does a 'rebase' happen?

Every 1 hour there is a change in supply called a 'rebase'. The rebase is automatically triggered by an online dashboard.

  • Negative rebases are amplified to make ForeverFOMO return to positive rebase territory faster. Rebases are triggered 24 times a day.

  • Every 1 hour ForeverFOMO will do a price rebase that will increase the price 10%.
